Black beans and rice

Link to recipe here.

Description

It's beans and rice.

Not only does it taste good, it is good for you.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 3 1/2 cups canned black beans, drained
  • 1 1/2 cups low sodium, low fat vegetable broth
  • 3/4 cup uncooked white rice
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped

Directions

  1. Heat oil in saucepan over medium-high heat. Add onion and garlic; cook and stir until onion has softened, about 4 minutes. Stir in rice to coat; cook and stir for two minutes.
  2. Add vegetable broth and bring to a boil. Cover, reduce to a simmer, and cook until liquid is absorbed, about 20 minutes.
  3. Stir in beans, cumin, and cayenne; cook until beans are warmed through.
