Black beans and rice

Link to recipe here.

Description

It's beans and rice.

Not only does it taste good, it is good for you.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 3 1/2 cups canned black beans, drained
  • 1 1/2 cups low sodium, low fat vegetable broth
  • 3/4 cup uncooked white rice
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Place potatoes into a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until tender, about 20 minutes. Drain and cut into cubes.
  3. Whisk mayonnaise, Parmesan cheese, Dijon mustard, vinegar, cayenne pepper, salt, black pepper, and paprika together in a large bowl.
  4. Add potatoes, red onion, and green onions; gently toss to combine.
